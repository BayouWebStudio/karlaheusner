The Story

About

From the vibrant newsrooms of Belize to the written page — Karla Marie Heusner's journey has always been one of storytelling in all its forms.

Karla Marie Heusner is a Belizean author, journalist, and educator whose writing bridges narrative nonfiction and cultural storytelling with precision and warmth. Rooted in the rich, layered history of Belize — where her family name graces the national hospital — she writes with a deep sense of place, identity, and belonging. Her acclaimed essay collection, "Food For Thought: Chronicles of Belize," distills decades of observation into witty, incisive portraits of Belizean life and culture.

With a career spanning news editing at The Reporter, television journalism at News Five / Channel 5 Belize, and a Master's degree in English, Karla now serves as an Adjunct English Instructor at Miami Dade College — nurturing the next generation of writers with the same love of language that has defined her own path. Though based in the Houston, Texas area, Belize remains forever in her heart and in her writing.
